Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Khoya (mawa)
  • 2 tablespoons All-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 teaspoon Baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons Milk (adjust as needed)
  • Oil or ghee for deep frying
  • For Sugar Syrup:
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1 cup Water
  • 4-5 Cardamom pods, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon Rose water

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, crumble khoya and knead it into a smooth dough, adding all-purpose flour and baking powder gradually.
  2. Add milk as needed to form a soft dough. Divide the dough into small equal-sized balls and roll them into smooth, crack-free balls.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Fry the gulab jamun balls on low to medium heat until golden brown and evenly cooked from all sides.
  4. In the meantime, prepare the sugar syrup by dissolving sugar in water over medium heat. Add crushed cardamom pods and simmer until the syrup thickens slightly.
  5. Once the gulab jamuns are fried, transfer them directly from the oil to the warm sugar syrup. Let them soak for at least 2 hours, allowing the jamuns to absorb the syrup and become soft and juicy.
  6. Finally, sprinkle rose water over the gulab jamuns before serving. Enjoy this delightful Indian dessert warm or chilled!
