Fruit Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups strawberries, sliced
  • 2 cups grapes, halved
  • 2 bananas, sliced
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh mint leaves, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine all the fruits.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together honey and lemon juice until well combined.
  3. Pour the honey-lemon mixture over the fruits and gently toss to coat.
  4. Sprinkle chopped mint leaves on top for garnish.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  6. Enjoy this refreshing and healthy fruit salad!
