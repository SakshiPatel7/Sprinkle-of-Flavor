Masala Papad

Ingredients:

  • 4 Papads
  • 1 Tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 Green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon Chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon Red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Roast the papads on an open flame or in a microwave until crisp.
  2. Mix chopped tomatoes, onions, green chili, and coriander leaves in a bowl.
  3. Add chaat masala, red chili powder, cumin powder, and salt to the chopped vegetables and mix well.
  4. Place the roasted papads on a serving plate.
  5. Spoon the prepared vegetable mixture evenly over each papad.
  6. Serve immediately.
