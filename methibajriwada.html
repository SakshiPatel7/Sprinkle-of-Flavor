Methi Bajri Wada

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Bajra flour (pearl millet flour)
  • 1/2 cup Methi (fenugreek leaves), finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Onion, finely chopped
  • 2 Green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 inch Ginger, grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine bajra flour, chopped methi, chopped onion, green chilies, grated ginger, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin seeds, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually to form a stiff dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into round wadas.
  4. Heat oil in a pan for frying.
  5. Once the oil is hot, carefully slide the wadas into the hot oil.
  6. Fry the wadas on medium heat until they turn golden brown and crispy from all sides.
  7. Remove the fried wadas from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  8. Serve hot with chutney or sauce.
