Rajma Chawal Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Rajma (Kidney beans), soaked overnight
  • 1 cup Basmati rice
  • 2-3 Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 Garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 inch Ginger, grated
  • 1-2 Green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon Red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon Coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons Oil or Ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. Pressure cook soaked rajma with salt until soft.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions, minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. Saut√© until onions turn golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add cooked rajma along with its water. Mix and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, cook basmati rice separately until fluffy.
  8. Serve hot rajma masala with steamed rice. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
