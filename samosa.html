Samosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • For the filling:
  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas, boiled
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, salt, and oil. Add water gradually to form a firm dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, ginger-garlic paste, and green chili. Saut√© for a minute.
  3. Add mashed potatoes, boiled peas, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes. Let the filling cool down.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each ball into a circle. Cut each circle in half.
  5. Take one half-circle, wet the edges with water, and form a cone shape. Fill the cone with potato filling and seal the edges.
  6. Repeat with the remaining dough and filling.
  7. Heat oil in a pan for frying. Fry the samosas until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Serve hot with mint chutney or tamarind chutney.
