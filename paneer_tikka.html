Paneer Tikka Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Add paneer cubes, diced bell pepper, and diced onion to the marinade. Mix until well coated.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  4. Preheat the grill or grill pan over medium heat.
  5. Thread the marinated paneer, bell pepper, and onion onto skewers.
  6. Grill the skewers for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the paneer and vegetables are charred and cooked through.
  7. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges. Enjoy your delicious Paneer Tikka!
