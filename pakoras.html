Vegetable Pakoras Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium potato, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, grated
  • 1/2 cup spinach leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine chickpea flour, water, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt to make a smooth batter.
  2. Add sliced onions, potatoes, grated carrots, chopped spinach, and cauliflower florets to the batter. Mix until all vegetables are coated evenly.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of the vegetable batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Remove the pakoras from the oil using a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  6. Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice.
