Chole Bhature Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Chickpeas (chole), soaked overnight
  • 2 Onions, finely chopped
  • 3 Tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 inch Ginger, grated
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 Green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons Coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder
  • 1 teaspoon Red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons Oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing
  • For Bhature:
  • 2 cups All-purpose flour
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon Baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with salt until soft.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add grated ginger, minced garlic, and chopped green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and oil separates.
  6. Now add turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and garam masala powder. Mix well.
  7. Add cooked chickpeas along with the water. Adjust the consistency of gravy as required.
  8. Cook covered on low heat for 10-15 minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  10. For Bhature:
  11. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, yogurt, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  12. Knead into a soft dough. Cover and let it rest for 2 hours.
  13. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into balls.
  14. Roll each ball into a circle of desired thickness.
  15. Heat oil in a pan for frying.
  16. Fry the rolled bhature in hot oil until golden brown and puffed.
  17. Drain excess oil and serve hot with chole.
