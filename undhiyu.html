Undhiyu Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Fenugreek leaves (methi leaves), plucked and washed
  • ½ cup Whole wheat flour (Chapati atta)
  • ½ cup Besan (gram flour)
  • 1 Green chilies chopped finely
  • 1 teaspoon Ginger paste or freshly grated or crushed
  • ¼ teaspoon Turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon Red chili powder
  • ¼ teaspoon Baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 ½ teaspoons Sugar
  • 2-3 teaspoons Lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons Oil
  • 2-3 tablespoons Water or more or less
  • Cooking oil spray for air-frying
  • ⅓ cup Desiccated coconut
  • ¼ cup Peanuts
  • 2 tablespoons Sesame seeds
  • 3 teaspoons Red chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons Coriander powder
  • ½ teaspoon Turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon Sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons Lemon juice or Lime juice
  • ½ cup Cilantro or coriander leaves
  • 4-5 Green chilies
  • ½ inch Ginger
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 5 tablespoons Oil
  • ½ teaspoon Cumin seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds)
  • ¼ teaspoon Hing (Asafoetida)
  • 4 Small baingan (baby eggplant)
  • 7-8 Baby potatoes
  • ½ cup Sweet potatoes peeled and cubed
  • ½ cup Green peas
  • ⅓ cup Purple yam (Kand or ratalu)
  • 1 cup Fresh tuvar dana (Green pigeon peas or lilva)
  • 1 cup Surti papdi
  • 1 to 1 ½ cups Water {1 cup for 6QT, 1 ½ cups for 8QT}

Instructions:

  1. Take wheat flour, besan, spices (turmeric, red chili powder), salt, sugar, baking soda, ginger paste, green chilies in a bowl. Mix well.
  2. Add oil and lemon juice. Mix well by rubbing between your fingers and thumb.
  3. Add methi leaves and mix.
  4. Now add little water at a time and knead into a smooth and soft dough. Don’t make it too soft otherwise, it becomes too sticky.
  5. Grease your palm with oil and take about 2 teaspoons of portion, shape into small oval-shaped dumplings. During the half-way through of shaping process, the dough may start to stick. Wash the hands, grease again and shape.
  6. While you’re shaping, preheat your air fryer to 400°F (200°C) for 5 minutes. Arrange the shaped methi muthia into the air fryer tray. Spray it lightly with cooking oil spray.
  7. Air fry for 10 minutes. Halfway through open and give it a shake for even browning all the sides. This time may vary depending on the size of your muthiya.
  8. Take dry masala stuff (dry coconut, peanuts, sesame seeds, salt, sugar, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder) in a food processor. Pulse it and make a semi-coarse powder.
  9. Add ginger, garlic, green chilies, cilantro and lemon(lime) juice. Again pulse and make a coarse, dry mixture.
  10. Remove it to a bowl and keep it ready.
  11. Lightly scrub the skin of the baby potatoes instead of peeling. Also, peeled and cubed the sweet potato.
  12. Make a cross ‘+’ sign at the bottom of the baby eggplant (careful not to cut into pieces) and stuff the center with prepared masala.
  13. Take all the veggies (except eggplant) in a large bowl. Add remaining masala and mix well.
  14. Heat the oil in instant pot on saute mode. Once hot add cumin seeds and ajwain. Let them sizzle a bit. Then add hing.
  15. Immediately add masala coated veggies, stuffed eggplants and air-fried methi muthia. Plus, add water and lightly mix.
  16. Cover the instant pot with a lid, keep the vent to sealing. Cook on manual (high pressure) for 6 minutes. After 5 minutes, do QPR. Once the pin drops, open the lid and surti undhiyu is ready to enjoy.
